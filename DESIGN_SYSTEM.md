# SENTINEL Design System

## 1. Product Philosophy
**"The city has a live multiplayer mode."**

SENTINEL is not a corporate SaaS dashboard or a crime reporting app. It is a **live urban awareness network** designed to feel like the social layer of the city. The app provides temporary, story-like reports of nearby activity in an environment that feels warm, organic, cinematic, and emotionally alive. 

## 2. Visual Direction
- **Vibe:** Cinematic, late-evening, atmospheric, warm, urban, soft, tactile.
- **Reference:** Tokyo night photography, Copenhagen lifestyle apps, Apple editorial photography, A24 film grading.
- **Avoid:** Neon cyberpunk, tech-bro visuals, hacker aesthetics, AI blue gradients, sterile glassmorphism.

## 3. Color Palette
The colors evoke an evening in the city—moody but warm.
- **Backgrounds:** Warm charcoal (`#18181A`), Muted graphite (`#232325`), Fog gray (`#2C2C2E`)
- **Accents:** 
  - Ambient Amber (`#F5A623`) - For activity and warmth
  - Deep Olive (`#4F5D4E`) - For safety and navigation
  - Muted Coral (`#E06D53`) - For temporary alerts (soft, not harsh)
- **Text & Highlights:** Warm cream (`#F8F6F0`), Soft sand (`#E5E0D8`)

## 4. Typography
- **Primary Font:** Inter (or SF Pro / Satoshi if available)
- **Style:** Highly readable, generous spacing, elegant, calm. No futuristic gaming fonts.
- **Usage:**
  - Headers: Medium/Semi-bold, tight tracking.
  - Body: Regular, generous line height for breathing room.

## 5. UI Components & Layout
- **Shapes:** Soft rounded corners (e.g., 20px - 24px border radii for cards).
- **Surfaces:** Subtle depth using soft shadows, slight borders, and muted transparency (blur without excessive glassmorphism).
- **Map:** Dark mode, styled softly (similar to Apple Maps at night). No classic pins—use floating glowing indicators, pulses, and heatmaps.
- **Navigation:** Floating Action Buttons (FABs) and ambient bottom navigation bars that feel tactile.

## 6. Motion Guidelines
- **Feel:** Apple-like, tactile, fluid, and calm.
- **Effects:** Soft inertia, cinematic fade-ins, gentle bounce, blur transitions.
- **Avoid:** Flashy, snappy, or gamer-like animations.

## 7. UX Experience
- **Focus:** Community awareness, local trust.
- **Interactions:** Lightweight, story-like. 
- Users share live moments without influencer culture or noisy feeds.
